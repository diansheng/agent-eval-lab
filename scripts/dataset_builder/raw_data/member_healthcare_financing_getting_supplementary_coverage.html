Healthcare financing
Consider whether you need supplementary coverage for healthcare

Before you buy any Integrated Shield Plan, CareShield Life and ElderShield Supplement plan, review your health insurance coverage to suit your healthcare preferences and financial circumstances.

  1. Overview
  2. Resources
  3. Articles
Overview
Understanding your supplementary coverage
Integrated Shield Plan

An Integrated Shield Plan combines MediShield Life with additional private insurance coverage offered by private insurers for higher ward classes or private hospitals.

/content/web/sg/en/member/healthcare-financing/getting-supplementary-coverage/integrated-shield-plan.html false
Find out more
CareShield Life and ElderShield Supplement plan

Supplement plans by private insurers complement your basic CareShield Life and ElderShield with additional benefits such as higher monthly payout amounts for long-term care.
