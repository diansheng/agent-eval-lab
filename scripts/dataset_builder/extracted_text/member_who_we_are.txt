Our Inclusivity
Serving with an inclusive mindset
As the champion of the nation’s retirement needs, we pride ourselves in providing disability-friendly services to our members which are designed and served by staff with an inclusive mindset.  We see in ourselves a team of committed professionals who come from different backgrounds, reflecting the diversity in our membership, contributing different perspectives and transformative ideas to deliver outstanding services to our members.  We believe that persons with disabilities can play meaningful roles and contribute in their own unique way. Together, we foster an inclusive and caring environment where every staff can develop and grow to achieve their aspirations.
