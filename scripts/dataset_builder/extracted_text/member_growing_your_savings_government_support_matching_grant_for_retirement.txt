Eligibility
Who is eligible?
There is no need to apply for the Matched Retirement Savings Scheme (MRSS). Your eligibility is automatically assessed every year.
We will notify eligible members via email or post generally by end February of the year. Find out if you are eligible via your
Retirement Dashboard
.
You will be eligible for MRSS for the year if you are a Singapore Citizen only, reside in Singapore and meet the following criteria

Getting a grant
How do I receive the matching grant?
If you are eligible for MRSS and have received cash top-ups in your Retirement or Special Account, you will receive the matching grant. Simply ensure that your one-time top-up via PayNow is submitted by 31st December or your recurring top-up via GIRO is submitted by 31st October in the year of eligibility. You must be a Singapore Citizen only at the point of grant crediting. Please note that cash top-ups which attract the matching grant of up to $2,000 a year will not be eligible for tax relief.
Who can make a cash top-up?
Anyone such as yourself, your loved ones, employers
(5)

How much do I have to top up?
You can top up any amount. You can choose to make a one-time top-up or regular small top-ups whenever you can.
false
For example:
Topping up $600 every year qualifies you for a $600 matching grant in the following year, increasing your RA savings by a total of $1,200.
Topping up $2,000 every year qualifies you for a $2,000 matching grant in the following year, increasing your RA savings by a total of $4,000.

For caregivers
Supporting Singaporeans with disabilities
As a caregiver of a person with disability, you can check your loved one's MRSS eligibility
(6)
and top up to their Retirement or Special Account (RA/SA) on their behalf. The matching grant will be automatically credited to their RA/SA at the beginning of the following year. You may view their MRSS eligibility and top-ups via their
Retirement Dashboard
.
(6)
Parents of a child with disability below 21 can check their child's eligibility via the Child Dashboard. Simply log in to
my cpf Home > Child Dashboard
