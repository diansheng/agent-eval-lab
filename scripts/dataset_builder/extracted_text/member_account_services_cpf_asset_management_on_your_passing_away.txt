Distribution
How CPF savings are distributed
Deceased has a valid CPF nomination
CPF savings will be distributed to the nominee(s). We will contact the nominees(s) within 10 working days after we are informed of the member's demise. The notification will include instructions on how to withdraw the savings from the deceased's CPF account.
https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/account-services/providing-for-your-loved-ones/making-a-cpf-nomination
false
Making a nomination is important, easy and ensures that your CPF savings will be distributed according to your wishes, at no cost, when you pass away.
CPF Nomination Scheme
Deceased did not have a valid CPF nomination
CPF savings will be transferred to the Public Trustee Office for distribution in line with the Singapore’s intestacy laws:
Intestate Succession Act if deceased is non-Muslim.
Administration of Muslim Law Act if deceased is Muslim.
false
How nominees receive the CPF savings
CPF savings will be automatically disbursed to nominees who meet the eligibility criteria
Automatic Disbursement
The CPF savings will be automatically paid to you, if you meet the following criteria, subject to additional eligibility requirements:
Are a Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident
Aged 18 and above
Have a local residential address
Have a registered bank account with CPF Board
No withdrawal application is required.
false

Savings in Ordinary, Special, MediSave and Retirement Accounts

Discounted Singtel shares (SDS)
A deceased member’s SDS will be distributed to the nominee(s) in the proportion indicated in the deceased member’s CPF nomination. The shares will either be transferred to the nominee’s personal CDP Account or sold, based on the nominee’s instructions.

Things to note
Other policies
Using MediSave to pay final hospitalisation bill
Member had signed a Medical Claims Authorisation Form (MCAF)
No action needed, MediSave and MediShield Life/Integrated Shield Plan will be automatically used to pay the final hospitalisation bill.
false
If no MCAF was signed
The deceased’s spouse, parents, child (aged 21 and above), or donee/deputy can sign the form at the hospital within two weeks of the member’s demise. This should be done as soon as possible, as hospitals have to submit a MediSave claim before the member’s CPF savings are distributed.
If there’s no immediate family or donee/deputy, other relatives of the deceased can sign the MCAF, with the approval of the Ministry of Health. The hospital can help you obtain this approval.
false
Withdrawing or claiming from other schemes
Claiming investments under CPF Investment Scheme (CPFIS)
Administrators/executors will need to contact the respective product provider (i.e. financial institution that sells investments) or agent bank (DBS, OCBC or UOB) to claim these investments and any cash balance in the CPF Investment Account.
false
Claiming under the Dependants’ Protection Scheme (DPS)
If the deceased was insured under the DPS, CPF Board will inform the insurer (Great Eastern Life). The insurer will then send claim application details to the DPS nominee(s)'s / deceased's address.
https://www.greateasternlife.com/sg/en/personal-insurance/our-products/life-insurance/dependants-protection-scheme.html
false
Great Eastern Life
Housing matters
Home Protection Scheme (HPS)
If an outstanding housing loan was insured by the deceased under the HPS, we will automatically assess the deceased’s claim eligibility once CPF Board is notified of the member’s death by the Immigration and Checkpoints Authority (ICA).
CPF Board will inform the co-owner(s) or next-of-kin of the outcome and if additional information is required.
When the claim is approved, the HPS claim benefits will be paid directly to the mortgagee, to settle the outstanding housing loan, up to the insured sum.
false
Waiver of CPF used for property
If the deceased had used CPF savings to buy a property, refund of the housing usage is waived automatically. The property will not be covered by CPF nomination and instead form part of the estate.
The deceased’s share in a property with a joint tenancy automatically passes to the remaining owner(s).
If there’s a tenancy-in-common or property was solely owned, the deceased's share will be inherited under their will if they had written one. Otherwise, intestacy laws will apply.
false
Education loans
Waiver for CPF Education Loan Scheme
If the deceased used CPF savings under the CPF Education Loan Scheme, there’s no need to repay either the CPF savings used or any accrued interest.
false
Terminating healthcare policies
MediShield Life/Integrated Shield Plan, CareShield Life
If the deceased was insured under MediShield Life/Integrated Shield Plan, the respective cover automatically ends. We will refund any unused premiums to the payer's MediSave Account. The unused period is the number of days remaining in the policy year from the date the member passed, excluding the day of their demise.
false
If the deceased was insured under CareShield Life, refunds to the payer's MediSave Account will be made only if the insured passes away within the first 60 days of the first policy year.
