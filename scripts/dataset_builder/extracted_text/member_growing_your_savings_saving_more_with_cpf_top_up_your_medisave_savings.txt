Things to note
Before you make a cash top-up
Eligibility
To make MediSave top-ups to yourself or a loved one,  you must be
A Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident
Aged 16 or older
false
Top-up limits
The amount of MediSave top-ups one can receive is capped at the Basic Healthcare Sum (BHS). When you make a MediSave top-up online, you will be shown the amount of MediSave top-up you can receive before you reach the top-up limit.
If you are making a top-up to your loved ones, please check with your recipient directly on the amount he can receive.
false
If you are self-employed
To make top-ups to yourself or a loved one, you must have fully settled or be on a monthly instalment plan for your MediSave payable under the Self-Employed Scheme.
Top-ups allocated to your MediSave Account cannot be used to offset your MediSave payable (if any).
For details, read more about the
Self-Employed Scheme
.
false
Top-ups are irreversible
Cash top-ups to MediSave Account are irreversible.
false
Cash top-ups
Who you can make a cash top-up to
1
Yourself and your loved ones
Receive up to $8,000 tax relief when you make a cash top-up to yourself and up to another $8,000 when you make a cash top-up to your loved ones (parents, parents-in-law, grandparents, grandparents-in-law, spouse and siblings) in each calendar year.
If your recipient is eligible for the Matched MediSave Scheme, the top-up amount which attracts the matching grant will not qualify for tax relief.
Tax relief only applies to givers and not recipients.
false
2
Any other CPF member (e.g. friends or other relatives)
Build up the CPF savings for the healthcare needs of people you care about by making a cash top up to their MediSave Account.
Top-ups made to others (e.g. friends or other relatives) are not eligible for tax relief.
false
MediSave top-ups through your employer or platform operator
Employers and platform operators can enjoy
dollar-for-dollar tax deductions* by making cash top-ups for you.
As employees and platform workers, you will receive tax relief of up to $8,000 from these cash top-ups, subject to certain
conditions
.
If you're eligible for the Matched MediSave Scheme, you'll also enjoy matching grants of up to $1,000 per year. Top-up amounts which attract the matching grant won't qualify for tax relief.

employee

platform worker
.
