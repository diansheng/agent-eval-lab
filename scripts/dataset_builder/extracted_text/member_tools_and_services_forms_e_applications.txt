Top up your 3 CPF accounts
This form allows you to make a top-up to your own CPF accounts (Ordinary Account, Special Account or Retirement Account, MediSave Account) or on behalf of another person.
CPF top-ups to your 3 accounts are not eligible for tax relief unless you are a self-employed person.
Please refer to this
FAQ
for more information on the tax relief eligibility of contributions by self-employed persons.
For self-employed persons, amounts allocated to your MediSave Account from top-ups made via this form will be used to offset your MediSave payable and meet the MediSave requirement for Workfare Income Supplement.

Earning higher returns
Withdraw CPF investments
You can apply to close your CPF Investment Account and cease your participation in the CPF Investment Scheme (CPFIS) if you meet the
requirements
to withdraw your CPFIS investments.
Upon successful application, your CPF Investment Account will be closed and the investments will be converted to cash investments and held in your own name. Any future proceeds from the sale of these investments will be paid directly to you in cash instead of to your CPF account.
The application link will appear on your Investment dashboard if you meet the requirements.
