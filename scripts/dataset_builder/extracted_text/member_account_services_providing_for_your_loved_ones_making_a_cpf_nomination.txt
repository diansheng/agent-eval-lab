Benefits
Why make a CPF nomination
1
You decide who gets your CPF savings
With a CPF nomination, your CPF savings will be distributed to your nominees,  according to your wishes.
false
Without a CPF nomination, your CPF savings will be distributed by the Public Trustee’s Office to your family members  based on the intestacy laws or Muslim Inheritance Certificate. You cannot decide which family member will receive your savings or how much they will receive.
2
Fast and convenient distribution of your CPF savings
A CPF nomination allows your nominees  to claim your savings quickly and conveniently.
false
Without a CPF nomination, it can take up to six months for the Public Trustee’s Office to identify which of your family members are eligible to claim your savings.
3
No extra costs to distribute your CPF savings to your nominees
Making a CPF nomination is free. Your nominees will not be required to pay any charges to claim your CPF savings when you pass on.
https://www.cpf.gov.sg/makecpfnom
false
Without a CPF nomination, the Public Trustee's Office will deduct an administrative fee from your CPF savings before distributing to your family members based on the intestacy laws or your Muslim Inheritance Certificate.

When you should make a CPF nomination
You should make a CPF nomination as soon as you start working and building up your CPF savings. In fact, you can make a nomination once you turn 16.

Upon marriage, any existing CPF nomination will be revoked. This allows you to make provisions taking into account the change in your family nucleus.

How it works
Understanding what’s involved in nominations
How nomination works
What a nomination covers
CPF savings* in your Ordinary, Special, MediSave, and Retirement Accounts
CPF LIFE premium balance
Discounted Singtel shares
* CPF savings cannot be included in your will because they do not form your estate. This arrangement protects your CPF savings from creditor claims on any outstanding debts you may have, and preserves your savings fully for your nominees or family members.
false
What isn’t covered under a nomination
Properties bought using your CPF savings
Payout from Dependants’ Protection Scheme (DPS)
Investments made under CPF Investment Scheme
false
Distribution of your CPF savings
Upon your demise, CPF Board will get in touch with your nominees to facilitate the distribution of your CPF savings. Your CPF savings will be distributed to them in cash.
If you don’t have a nomination, your CPF savings will be transferred to the Public Trustee’s Office for them to distribute to your beneficiaries in accordance with the intestacy laws.

What to consider
Things to note before making a nomination
Who should receive your CPF savings and how much
You’ll need to decide on your nominees and the percentage of your CPF savings each nominee should receive.
You can specify anyone as a nominee, including people who don’t have a CPF account.
false
Witnesses for your nomination
Disputes on who should receive your CPF savings may sometimes arise after you have passed on. That is why you have to appoint two witnesses who can attest that you have made your nomination willingly and consciously. Your witnesses cannot be your nominee or an organisation.
false
You can view the
video guide
for more information on being a witness for a CPF nomination online.
Allowing your loved ones to access your CPF account information upon demise
To bring greater convenience to you and your loved ones, your nominees and eligible family members under intestacy laws will be able to access your CPF account information upon your demise.
This will make it easier for your loved ones to reconcile your CPF account information and settle post-demise matters relating to your CPF account.
Your CPF account information remains confidential while you are alive.

Making a CPF nomination is a simple and easy process
Nominate online
You’ll need:
Your Singpass
Your nominees’ NRIC or Foreign Identification Number (FIN), mailing address, and email address
Two witnesses, their NRIC or FIN, mailing address, email address and Singapore-registered mobile phone number
