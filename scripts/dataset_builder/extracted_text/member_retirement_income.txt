Retirement income
Monthly payouts
We cannot predict how long we'll live. Hence it’s not easy to decide how much we need for our retirement.  Spend too much and you might not have enough. Save too much and you end up depriving yourself.
That's why we launched CPF LIFE. A scheme that provides you with monthly payouts no matter how long you live. With CPF LIFE, you can fully enjoy the savings you've built, with greater peace of mind.
The monthly payouts you’ll receive depends on how much retirement savings you have.

Moving to a 3-room or smaller flat and receive a bonus
If you are moving to a 3-room or smaller flat, you can supplement your retirement income by committing your housing refunds towards your CPF monthly payouts, and receive a Silver Housing Bonus of up to $40,000 in cash.

Ageing in place and monetise your housing property
If you own a
HDB flat
You can sell part of your flat’s lease to HDB through the Lease Buyback Scheme, and use the proceeds to top up your CPF Retirement Account to boost your CPF monthly payouts in retirement, while continuing to live in your flat. You can also receive a cash bonus of up to $30,000.
More details
false
If you own a
private property
You can consider speaking with your financial institution or advisor on the available products in the market that allow you to monetise your private property to supplement your retirement income while ageing in place. As with all important financial decisions, please understand the nature of the product, and assess your risk tolerance and retirement needs before deciding if it is appropriate for you.
You may use
HDB’s Enquire Housing Monetisation Options e-Service
to check your eligible Housing Monetisation options.

Retirement withdrawals
Withdrawals for immediate needs
If you have an immediate need, you can withdraw up to $5,000 after 55. You can withdraw more, if you have set aside the required retirement sum in your Retirement Account.
Learn more
Early retirement
Withdrawing on reduced life expectancy
If you have been certified by an accredited doctor to have a reduced life expectancy, you can apply to withdraw some of your CPF savings.

Cash payouts for the elderly
Eligible seniors aged 65 and above will automatically receive payouts every three months. This benefits Singaporeans who had low incomes through life and now have little or no family support.
