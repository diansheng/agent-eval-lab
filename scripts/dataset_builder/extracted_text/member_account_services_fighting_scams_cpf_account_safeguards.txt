Overview
Key measures to better partner you in safeguarding your CPF savings

Online CPF withdrawals can be instantly disabled at any time with
3 simple steps
.

Daily withdrawal limit
Set a limit to determine the maximum daily amount you can withdraw online. The default is $2,000, and the maximum is $50,000.

Prevention
Safeguard your CPF savings with these preventive measures today
Ensure account settings are updated
Keep contact details up-to-date so that you receive timely notifications whenever transactions are made.
Use only your personal bank account for withdrawals from your CPF savings.
Keep your daily withdrawal limit low.
Activate the CPF Withdrawal Lock to disable online CPF withdrawals if not withdrawing soon.
Consider reviewing and updating your details in your Account settings regularly, especially when prompted by CPF Board.

Beware of common scam signs
Impersonation scams
Beware of scammers who:
Pretend to be CPF or other Government officials.
Claim that your Singpass was used for committing crimes (e.g. buying a SIM card) and you are under investigation for money laundering.
Pressure you to share personal information or hand over valuables such as cash, gold bars and jewellery for investigation.
false
Investment scams
Avoid advertisements where:
Investment opportunities are too good to be true, promising risk-free high returns.
Well-known personalities or Government officials make endorsements.
You’re pressured to transfer money immediately or miss the opportunity.
false
Tell-tale signs of scam
Look out for messages that:
Come from sources you do not recognise.
Prompt you to click unknown links or download files or apps on your devices.
Request to control your device by screen-sharing.
For more info on other scam types, please visit
