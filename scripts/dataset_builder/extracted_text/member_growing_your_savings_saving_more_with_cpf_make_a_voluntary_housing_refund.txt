Eligibility
Who is it for?
You can make a voluntary housing refund if you’ve used your Ordinary Account (OA) savings for your property.
Check OA usage
Benefits
Lesser OA usage, More for retirement
Less amount needs to be refunded to your CPF savings when selling your property.
The earlier you refund your CPF savings, the less you’ll need to refund upon the sale or transfer of the property.
Keep in mind that the mandatory amount to refund includes funds used to pay for the property and any interest you would’ve earned if the funds had remained in your CPF account.
