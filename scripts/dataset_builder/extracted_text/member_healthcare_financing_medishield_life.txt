About
What you need to know about MediShield Life
Lifelong coverage
With MediShield Life, you’ll remain covered for life, regardless of your age or health condition.
false
Better protection
MediShield Life helps you pay for large medical bills. This means you use less MediSave or cash, especially for B2 and C ward types.
false
Eligibility
Who’s covered under MediShield Life
All Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents are automatically covered.
Benefits
Three ways MediShield Life protects you
Hospitalisation or day surgery
Reduce your cash expense for large hospital bills.
false
Outpatient treatment
Including expensive, recurring outpatient treatments, such as kidney dialysis, cancer drugs, and radiotherapy.
false
Long-term care
You’re covered for costs incurred in community hospitals and inpatient palliative care services.
false
How payouts work
A MediShield Life payout will be used to offset part of your bill. The remaining amount can be paid by MediSave (up to certain limits) and/or cash.
